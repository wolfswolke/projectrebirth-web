Welcome to Project Rebirth

About Us

We are a passionate group of developers and gamers dedicated to reviving games that have been killed of by their developers.

We do this with our self made tools and by reverse engineering game code.

Explore our website to learn more about our current projects. Join our community on Discord to connect with fellow gamers and stay updated on our latest developments.

There is currently a Petion going on to stop developers from killing games and leaving them dead.

Please take a look and help them out!
